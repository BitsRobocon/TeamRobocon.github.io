About Us

We are a group of enthusiasts who design and build robots to compete in various competitions.

What We Do

At Team Robocon BITS Pilani we take on challenges in the form of competitions to continuously learn and hone our skills.

Current Competitions

We are participating in 2 competitions of IIT Bombay techfest namely Operation Rahat and International Robotics Challenge.

flight_takeoff

Operation Rahat

As the reel of time unwinds in front of nova’s eyes, it seems as if death and destruction have become a daily trend with calamities and disasters every now and then. What if there was a device that could reach out to humans stuck in a disaster and help them providing First-Aid medical kits and food, saving thousands of lives. Aim is to design a drone which would help accomplish Nova’s dream and prove to be a saviour for the humanity.

commute

International Robotics Challenge

Since 1998, with the motto of spreading science and technology, Techfest has evolved to give an International platform to all the tech-enthusiasts for proving their calibre in science and technology. The zonal qualifying rounds for the competition will be held in various countries, whose winning entries will be invited to compete in the IRC Grand Finale to be held at Techfest 2018-19, IIT Bombay.
